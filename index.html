Here We AreLocation

Hoshiarpur


June 2019 , saw the launch of Click Salvager’s operations in Hoshiarpur, in partnership with Platform Tech Solutions. Click Salvager will launch several dry waste donation and home composting drives with the help of volunteers in Hoshiarpur. Soon, we will be delivering ‘Zero Waste’ services through a waste-picker cooperative model in villages near Hoshiarpur. For the first time in India, a low-cost and highly effective composter is designed and deployed in residential communities across the city.

JOIN US

AS A CITIZEN

CLICK SALVAGER strongly believes in the vital role that citizen communities play in creating zero-waste neighborhoods. In turn, we offer a suite of affordable and sustainable partner-led services for citizen communities.Citizens and communities are vital stakeholders in solving the urban waste management crisis.

AS CORPORATE OFFICIAL

Corporate Social Responsibility is a great opportunity for organizations to invest their people, expertise, and resources in addressing the key issues faced by us as a society. CLICK SALVAGER works closely with several corporate entrepreneurs whose impact footprint is spread across thousands of communities all over India.

AS GOVERNMENT PARTNER

At CLICK SALVAGER, we work with municipalities and government establishments to reimagine solid waste management systems. Personnel skills and administrative system capacities will be developed, along with creating the partnership frameworks that are necessary to collaborate with stakeholders like waste-pickers, social businesses, and citizens.

AS NGO PARTNER

CLICK SALVAGER provides a platform to Non-government organisations for socio-economic support along with social cause. They will have direct contact with the private and government sector those will provide funds in the form of donations and employment to them.

AS NSS PARTNER

CLICK SALVAGER provides a network of NSS groups along with others with the help of which all NSS groups of colleges get encourage for their task as this platform gives face to all NSS candidates online. They can get funds from other networks and be a part of this social cause.

AS A SERVICE PROVIDER

CLICK SALVAGER has transformed several social enterprises in the waste management sector.Upon joining hands with us, you become part of a diverse ecosystem of social entrepreneurs, change-makers, and enablers who are actively transforming the waste management landscape around us.
